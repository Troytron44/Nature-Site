Habitat

Iguanas inhabit rainforests, lowlands, swamps, deserts, rocks, and trees. Their diet includes fruits, flowers, leaves, insects, and snails

Green Iguana

The most abundant type is the green iguana (Iguana iguana). They can grow up to 6.6 feet in length and weigh up to 11 pounds. Their green color helps them blend into the undergrowth, and they have a row of spines on their body for defense.

Diverse Species

There are about 30 species of iguanas, each with unique adaptations. Some, like the Fiji banded iguana, are bright green with white or light blue bands, while others have more subdued colors
